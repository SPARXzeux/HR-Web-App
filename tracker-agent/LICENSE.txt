1. WHAT THIS APPLICATION IS

DelCargo Tracker ("the Application") is a companion desktop application to
the DelCargo HR web dashboard. It allows an employer (HR/Admin) to enable
periodic desktop screenshot capture for employees who use it, strictly for
workplace attendance and productivity oversight during active, employee-
initiated work shifts.

2. WHAT THE APPLICATION DOES

  a. The Application runs quietly in the background (system tray / menu
     bar) on the computer where it is installed.
  b. It periodically checks with the DelCargo HR server whether screen
     tracking has been turned on for the connected employee account.
  c. Screenshots are ONLY captured when ALL of the following are true:
       - HR/Admin has explicitly enabled tracking for that employee's
         account on the dashboard, AND
       - The employee has manually clocked in / started an active shift
         on their Timesheet Tracker.
  d. Captured screenshots are uploaded to DelCargo's PocketBase server and
     are visible to HR/Admin roles on the web dashboard. They are retained
     for 30 days by default and then automatically deleted, unless the
     employer has excluded a specific employee from automatic deletion.
  e. The Application does not record keystrokes, audio, webcam video, or
     browsing history, and does not run when tracking is off or no shift
     is active.

3. YOUR CONSENT

By installing and connecting this Application to your own DelCargo HR
account (using a setup code issued to you by HR/Admin), you acknowledge
and agree that:

  a. Your employer may capture periodic screenshots of your screen while
     you are on an active, tracked shift, as described above.
  b. You should only ever use a setup code that belongs to your own
     account. The Application will show you which email address a setup
     code resolves to before connecting, and will ask you to confirm this
     is you — never connect using a coworker's setup code.
  c. You may disconnect this Application from your account at any time
     ("Disconnect this computer" in the app), which stops all future
     screenshot capture from that device immediately.
  d. Questions about your employer's specific monitoring policy, data
     retention, or workplace consent requirements under local law should
     be directed to your HR/Admin, not to the software itself.
