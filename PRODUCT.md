# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Employees, HR Managers, and CEOs/Admins. They need a simple, structured, and highly legible interface to manage daily operations, salaries, and leave requests.

## Product Purpose
A robust, static-exported HR Operations portal supporting employee onboarding, dynamic team assignments, internal salary ledger/payroll checks, and multi-tier leave approval status machines.

## Brand Personality
Clean, professional, and modern. It should evoke trust, structural clarity, and efficient utility.

## Anti-references
Vibe-coded designs, unprofessional or sloppy layouts, cluttered structures, and hard-to-navigate flows. Avoid neon SaaS gradient clichés, overly rounded corners (>16px on cards), and ghost-card combinations (border + large soft shadows).

## Design Principles
- **Structural Clarity First**: The interface serves a functional workflow; layout rhythm and spacing must make the organizational hierarchy obvious at a glance.
- **Intentional Restraint**: Warm/cool-tinted neutrals, crisp typography, and focused accent usage to prevent cognitive load.
- **Frictionless Interaction**: Fast transitions (<200ms) and tactile scale-on-press feedback so the app feels immediate and responsive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Follow general clean accessibility patterns (good contrast ratios, readable line lengths of 65-75ch, keyboard navigability, and prefers-reduced-motion alternatives).
